Value that we create for our Clients:

Our Methodology:

We break the Analytics journey into more detailed iterative steps which we call as the AyamiH Analytics Life Cycle.

  • Identify the Client's Business Needs:

    Our Business Advisors liase with the Clients Team to identify the Opportunities of our Client. We define the scope, market conditions and goals related to the business question that our Client wants to solve, which will lead to the selection of one or more modelling method.

  • Prepare the Data:

    Wes McKinney, the author of Python for Data Analysis, defines Data Preparation as “cleaning, munging, combining, normalizing, and reshaping, slicing, dicing, and transforming data for analysis.”

    At AyamiH, depending on the business requirements of the Client and proposed analysis methods, this step involves using one or many of the above methods to clean and prepare the data for optimal results.

  • Explore the Data:

    This is where we explore the Data in an interactive and visual manner to identify the relevant variables, trends and relationships.

  • Create Models:

    In this step, our skilled team at AyamiH builds the model using quantitative methods, data mining and text mining methods to determine the model that produces the best results.

  • Validate Model:

    Once built, this selected model is tested and validated against your Data.

  • Deploy Model:

    After the Approval for use in production, the model is applied to new data to generate predictive insights.

  • Monitor and Assess the Models:

    The predictive performance of the model is monitored to ensure it is up to date and delivering valid results. If the model performance degrades, it’s time to make changes. When it no longer works or serves a business need, it is archived
